# Support

## Getting Help

If you need help with Tencent Meeting Video Downloader, here are the available channels:

- **GitHub Discussions**: for general questions, feedback, and ideas, visit the [Discussions](https://github.com/ChanMeng666/tencent-meeting-video-downloader/discussions) tab.
- **GitHub Issues**: for bug reports and feature requests, [open an issue](https://github.com/ChanMeng666/tencent-meeting-video-downloader/issues).

## Before You Ask

- Check the [README](README.md) for setup and usage instructions.
- Review the [Contributing Guide](CONTRIBUTING.md) for development and build steps.
- Search existing [issues](https://github.com/ChanMeng666/tencent-meeting-video-downloader/issues) and [discussions](https://github.com/ChanMeng666/tencent-meeting-video-downloader/discussions) — your question may already be answered.

## Scope of Support

This is an open-source project maintained on a volunteer basis. We do our best to respond, but
cannot guarantee response times. Please be patient and respectful.

## Security Issues

For security-related concerns, **do not open a public issue**. Follow the process in
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
